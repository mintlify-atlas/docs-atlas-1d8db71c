# Welcome to CADS

CADS (Cien Años De Soledad) is a specialized command-line tool for cleaning and processing literary text files. Built with Deno, it removes common artifacts like headers, footers, page numbers, and Roman numerals while preserving the natural flow and structure of your text.

## Why CADS?

When working with digitized literary texts, OCR scans, or PDF conversions, you often encounter repetitive artifacts that interfere with readability and text analysis:

- **Headers and footers** appearing on every page
- **Page numbers** breaking up sentences and paragraphs
- **Chapter markers** and Roman numerals cluttering the text
- **Formatting inconsistencies** from the scanning process

CADS automates the cleanup process, saving hours of manual editing and producing clean, readable text files ready for analysis, archival, or republishing.

## How It Works

CADS uses sophisticated regex patterns to identify and remove common text artifacts while preserving the semantic structure of your documents:

1. **Pattern Recognition** — Identifies headers, footers, and page numbers based on position and repetition
2. **Context-Aware Cleaning** — Determines whether to replace artifacts with spaces, line breaks, or remove them entirely
3. **Sentence Preservation** — Maintains proper sentence boundaries and paragraph structure
4. **Output Generation** — Creates cleaned files in a dedicated output directory

<Note>
CADS is specifically designed for literary and narrative texts. For technical documents or structured data formats, you may need to customize the cleaning patterns.
</Note>
